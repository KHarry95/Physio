Founders

Aggelos Sipitanos

Physiotherapist

Born in 1978 and a graduate of the Department of Physiotherapy from the Technological Educational Institute of Thessaloniki since 2005, he has many years of experience from working in the largest rehabilitation centers and as a partner in numerous clinics. From offering his services to patients as well as to athletes in clinics and intensive care units, he has specialized in a variety of treatments such as PNF, MANUAL THERAPY, MCKENZIE, MULLIGAN, KINESIOTAPING. He is a member of EFEA (Hellenic Physiotherapy Association of Pain) and he specializes in Biomedical Acupuncture and Dry Needling (Myofascial Release using Dry Needling). Since August 2011, he has been collaborating with his brother Dimitris Sipitanos, in the center of Thessaloniki, at the Physiotherapy Laboratory "KINESIS PHYSICAL THERAPY CENTER", combining clinical experience and knowledge in the service of patients.

Dimitris Sipitanos

Physiotherapist

Born in 1975 and a graduate of the Department of Physiotherapy of the National Gymnastics Academy of Bulgaria in Sofia since 1999, he has many years of experience from his work in the largest rehabilitation centers and as a partner of numerous clinics (Intensive Treatment Unit, in Orthopedics and Neurosurgical cases). He is a member of EFEA (Hellenic Physiotherapy Association of Pain) and specializes in Biomedical Acupuncture and Myofascial Release with Dry Needling. He specializes also in therapeutic techniques such as Mulligan Concept, Kinesiotaping etc. Since August 2011, he has been collaborating with his brother Aggelos Sipitanos, in the center of Thessaloniki, at the Physiotherapy Laboratory "KINESIS PHYSICAL THERAPY CENTER", combining clinical experience and knowledge in the service of patients.
