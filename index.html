Who we are

Through our many years of clinical experience, we are able to offer comprehensive recovery programs. We assess your situation, explain your problem, and through treatment, provide you with maximum benefit in a short period of time. We provide solutions for urgent issues and help you form habits that promote your recovery. With ongoing care, we implement ever-evolving treatment strategies tailored to your needs. In this effort, we have knowledge, experience and modern equipment.

Objectives of the workshop are:

  • The detailed assessment and explanation of the patient's problem.
  • The management and treatment of injuries and diseases that are accompanied by movement and functional disorders, impairment of strength or disability.
  • Improving the patient's quality of life and dealing with the daily difficulty caused by chronic pain.
  • The formation of habits that promote the recovery of the patient and avoiding any recurrence in the future.

We are cooperating with the National Agency for the Provision of Health Services.
